AI Summarizer Assistant

A WordPress plugin that adds a floating sidebar assistant to summarize your articles using GPT or Gemini.

Features

Floating Sidebar Button – Always visible on the right side of the editor.

Two AI Models – Choose between ChatGPT or Google Gemini.

Auto-Summarization – Automatically retrieves your full post content and opens the selected AI tool with a ready-made summarization prompt.

Editor Support – Works with both Gutenberg (Block Editor) and Classic Editor.

Installation

Download the ai-summarizer-assistant folder.

Upload it to your WordPress wp-content/plugins/ directory.

Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress.

Usage

Open the Post Editor (Add New or Edit Post).

Look for the floating chat icon on the right side.

Click the icon to open the modal.

Select GPT Summarizer or Gemini Summarizer.

A new tab will open with your article content automatically preloaded for summarization.

Requirements

WordPress 5.0+

Modern web browser
